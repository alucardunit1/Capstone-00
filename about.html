About Cody's Cars

Welcome to Cody's Cars! Where Reliability is Optional, But Fun is Guaranteed! We are a family-owned business with over 20 years of experience in the automotive industry. Our mission is to provide high-quality vehicles at competitive prices while delivering exceptional customer service.

Our History

Cody's Cars was founded in 2003 by Justin Estes. Starting with a small lot and a handful of cars, Cody's passion for automobiles and commitment to customer satisfaction quickly grew the business. Today, we have a large inventory of both new and used cars, and a team of dedicated professionals ready to assist you.

Our Values

  1. Integrity: We believe in honest and transparent dealings with our customers.
  2. Quality: We ensure that every vehicle we sell meets our high standards of quality and reliability.
  3. Customer Satisfaction: Our goal is to exceed your expectations and make your car-buying experience enjoyable.
